# 🧮 Math Challenge for Kids! 🎉

A fun and interactive math quiz game designed for kids! Test your arithmetic skills with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division problems — and race against the clock to score as many points as possible.

## Features

- **3 Difficulty Levels**
  - ⭐ **Easy** — Addition & subtraction with numbers 1–10
  - ⭐⭐ **Medium** — Addition & subtraction with numbers 1–50
  - ⭐⭐⭐ **Hard** — All four operations (including multiplication & division) with numbers 1–100

- **10 questions per round** with a **30-second timer** per question
- **Speed bonus** — answer faster to earn more points!
- **Instant feedback** after every answer
- **Star rating** at the end based on your performance
- **Keyboard support** — press Enter to submit your answer

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser — no installation needed!
2. Choose your difficulty level.
3. Type your answer in the box and click **✅ Check!** (or press **Enter**).
4. Try to answer as many questions correctly as fast as you can.
5. See your score and star rating at the end, then play again!
